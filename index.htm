About us

After exhausting ourselves talking about the publishing industry pitfalls-the depressing royalty rates, the unbreakable barrier set for newcomers, the utterly incomprehensible contracts, the unreasonable demands for control over the product, just to name a handful-we decided to take matters in on our hands and, following the popular phrase, be the change we wanted to see in the world.

The Writers’ Cabinet came to life as an act of revolt against conventional publishing and the royalty-oriented industry. We want good literature, more choices, new voices. We want art! The Great Story is our Cabinet’s first, second, and third priority. We want to bring interesting, beautiful, inspiring, breathtaking, intense, wild stories to the world.
